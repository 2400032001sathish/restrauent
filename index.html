About Our Restaurant

Our Story

Missamma Family Restaurant is a beloved local eatery in Chirala, serving authentic Andhra Pradesh cuisine with passion and tradition. Founded with the vision to bring authentic home-cooked flavors to everyone's table.

Our specialties include aromatic biryanis, flavorful fish curries, and traditional tandoori dishes. Each dish is prepared with the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods passed down through generations.

With a 4.2-star rating from 698 satisfied customers, we're proud to be a favorite dining destination for families in Chirala. "Best veg and non veg food in Chirala town!" - Customer Review
